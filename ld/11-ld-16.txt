 <br>

 <br>

A modern materialist finds himself in a curious position. With a certain degree of his success a mental activity can be reduced to a physical activity.<br>

 <br>

 <br>
Answer:<br>
The most essential characteristic of mind is memory. No, memory doesn't survive a person's death. Memory is clearly connected with a certain kind of brain structure, and since this structure decays at death memory also must cease.<br>

 <br>

Grammar<br>
used to<br>
'Used to + infinitive' is used to talk about a repeated action, state or situation in the past which has now changed.<br>
My brother used to be a footballer, but he is too old now.<br>
 <br>
